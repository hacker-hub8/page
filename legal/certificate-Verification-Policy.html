Certificate Verification Policy

Effective Date: August 2, 2025

  • Unique Certificate ID: Each certificate issued by HackerHub8 contains a unique alphanumeric ID for easy and secure verification.
  • Verification Portal: Certificates can be verified through our official page: hackerhub8.in/check-certificate.
  • Authenticity Guarantee: All certificates are digitally signed and encrypted to prevent forgery and unauthorized duplication.
  • Eligibility Criteria: Certificates are issued only after successful completion of internships, workshops, challenges, or validated contributions to the community.
  • Public Validation: Verifiers (HRs, institutions, government bodies) can check certificate authenticity by entering the ID or scanning the embedded QR code.
  • Types of Certificates: We issue the following:
    • Internship Completion Certificates
    • Workshop Participation Certificates
    • Cybersecurity Challenge Certificates
    • Mentorship & Volunteering Acknowledgements
  • Blockchain-Ready (Coming Soon): We are planning to integrate blockchain-based certificate issuance for immutable trust and decentralization.
  • Certificate Misuse: Any attempt to forge, edit, or use certificates without participation will lead to a permanent ban from the community and legal action.
  • Record Maintenance: All certificates are stored in our secure cloud database with date of issue, name, ID, and event details for 5+ years.
  • Certificate Lookup Support: If you lose your certificate ID, email us with your full name, registered email, and event details at certificates@hackerhub8.in.
  • Verification for Employers: HR departments or academic coordinators can contact us for bulk or official validation requests.
  • Zero Manual Intervention: Our verification system is fully automated and real-time, ensuring instant validation without human dependency.
  • Certificate Details: Each certificate includes: Name, Event Title, Date, Duration, Skill Highlights, and a Unique ID.
  • Privacy Compliant: We do not expose your certificate details unless a valid verification request is made through our portal.
  • Mentorship Verification: Mentors who contribute to HackerHub8 are issued verifiable proof of contribution with digital timestamps.
  • Anti-Abuse Checks: We regularly audit certificate requests and IP logs to ensure no manipulation or scripted abuse occurs.
  • Revocation Rights: We reserve the right to revoke any certificate if post-issuance fraud or policy violation is discovered.
