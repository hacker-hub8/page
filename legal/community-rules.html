HackerHub8 Community Rules

Last Updated: August 2, 2025

  • Respect All Members: Treat every member with dignity and respect. Harassment, hate speech, and discrimination are strictly prohibited.
  • Ethical Hacking Only: HackerHub8 promotes legal and ethical cybersecurity practices. Do not engage in or promote illegal hacking activities.
  • No Unauthorized Access: Sharing, teaching, or attempting unauthorized penetration of systems or devices is not tolerated.
  • Use Tools Responsibly: All tools and scripts provided are for educational and testing purposes only — not for exploitation or illegal use.
  • Privacy Matters: Do not post or share anyone’s personal information (doxxing) or sensitive data without consent.
  • Zero Tolerance for Piracy: Sharing cracked software, pirated tools, or license bypass methods is strictly forbidden.
  • Academic Integrity: Do not misuse community help for cheating in exams, assignments, or certifications.
  • Be Constructive: Provide helpful feedback, ask thoughtful questions, and engage positively with others.
  • Follow Platform Etiquette: Use proper channels like threads, categories, or tags when communicating in Discord, Telegram, or Forums.
  • No Spam or Promotions: Avoid excessive self-promotion, referral links, or irrelevant advertising unless permitted by admins.
  • Follow Admin Guidelines: Respect all moderators and follow any additional rules posted in specific channels or updates.
  • Report Violations: Help keep the community clean and safe by reporting suspicious or harmful behavior to the admin team.
  • Responsible Tool Sharing: Share scripts and tools only with proper documentation, usage limits, and ethical warnings.
  • Company & College Ethics: If you're participating via company or college, abide by their codes of conduct along with HackerHub8’s policies.
  • One Account Per User: Avoid creating multiple accounts to bypass rules or impersonate others.
  • Certification Honesty: Do not fake or manipulate certificate IDs or attempt to forge HackerHub8 credentials.
  • Learning Environment: This is a safe learning space for all levels — no shaming beginners or mocking doubts.
  • Age Policy: Members must be at least 16 years old or have permission if younger, depending on local laws.
  • Consequences: Violating rules may result in a warning, temporary ban, or permanent removal from the community.
