Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: August 2, 2025

  • Workshop Bookings: Fees paid for cybersecurity workshops (online or offline) are non-refundable once the registration is confirmed and slots are assigned.
  • Internship Fees: If you apply for a paid internship or hands-on project, once the payment is made and access is granted, the amount is non-refundable.
  • Tool & Resource Purchases: Digital products (like tools, scripts, templates) are non-returnable. Please read descriptions carefully before purchasing.
  • Accidental Payments: In case of double payments or accidental transfers, a refund request must be made within 48 hours to support@hackerhub8.in.
  • Cancellation Before Start: If a student cancels their workshop registration at least 5 days before the session, 50% refund may be processed on valid request.
  • Event Cancellations by HackerHub8: In case a workshop or event is cancelled from our side, 100% of the fee will be refunded to all paid participants.
  • Processing Time: Refunds (if approved) will be processed within 7–10 working days through the original mode of payment.
  • Non-Transferable: Paid registrations are not transferable to another person or future event, unless clearly mentioned during the booking.
  • Corporate Bookings: In case of B2B or institutional partnerships, refund terms will be mutually agreed upon and documented via MOU or agreement.
  • Change of Mind: No refunds will be issued for cancellations based on personal decisions or changing your mind after payment.
  • No Partial Refunds: We do not offer partial refunds for mid-program exits, late joins, or missed sessions during internships or workshops.
  • Right to Deny: HackerHub8 reserves the right to deny refund requests that are abusive, fraudulent, or violate our fair usage terms.
  • Refund Communication: All refund and cancellation communications must be done through email with proper details and screenshots of payment.
